Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment or can I walk in?

Walk‑ins are welcome, but booking ahead guarantees your spot and reduces wait time.

How long does a typical cut take?

A classic cut or fade usually takes 30‑45 minutes; more detailed services may run up to an hour.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, and mobile payments like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Do you offer services for kids?

Yes, our Kids Cut is designed for ages 5‑12, with a friendly approach to keep them comfortable.

Any tips for my first visit?

Come with clean, dry hair, bring reference photos if you have a specific style, and arrive a few minutes early to settle in.
